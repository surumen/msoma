Who we are

M-Soma is a training program for Kenyan high school graduates who aspire to become software developers. We operate as a non profit to ensure that the brightest minds in Kenya get access to great opportunities.

Â 

Our Mission

To provide young people from under-resourced communities and schools with the skills and community support they need to become professional software developers.
