Computer science is an essential part of every industry today. This program will prepare you to design and develop fascinating technology. You will also learn how to use this technology to innovative businesses across all industries. Upon graduation you will be prepared to work as an AI developer, app developer, software engineer, business innovator, and much more. Our computer science curriculum is designed with guidance from the best professors around the world in schools like the University of California - Berkeley, Stanford, MIT, among many others.

We are committed to empowering young Kenyans with the knowledge, resources and confidence they need to create a better future - for themselves, and their communities. Together with our partners, we are building a world where everyone has a chance to prosper. Where the doors of progress and opportunity are wide open.

We partner with a wide-range of educational institutions, foundations, corporate partners, and individuals who share in our vision of nurturing a new generation of leaders.
